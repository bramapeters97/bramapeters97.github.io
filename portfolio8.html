Understanding Illness: Data-Driven Insights into Dutch Social Security

When sick people face life-changing medical assessments, they deserve accuracy, care, and transparency. Our work at UWV helps monitor and improve this process — because no one should fall through the cracks of the system meant to protect them. I developed a monitoring dashboard for UWV's Sociaal-Medische Zaken division to provide intuitive information on medical assessment performance and WIA-benefits attribution by insurance doctors and social security services for clients (partially) unable to work.
