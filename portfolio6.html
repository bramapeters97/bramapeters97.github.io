Raspberries

A newly envisioned Digital Shopping Assistant for Rodebjer
that lets the customer decide when, and what for, they need help.

A new way to shop

The present-day shopping experience can be stressful and aggravating to some. Especially for introverts shopping may be challenging for a number of reasons, such as invasive staff, crowded clothing stores and a lack of self-efficacy. What about letting the customer decide when they need assistance?

The Digital Shopping Assistant helps them do just that. The digital help desk bridges the gap between customers and salespeople by letting the customer decide when, and what for, they need help.
